🌍 EarthQuakeWatch

A real-time earthquake monitoring platform that brings event data, ShakeMap generation, and operational alerts into one workflow.

It helps teams quickly understand what happened, where it happened, and what to do next.

Open ShakeMap View Events
What We Do

We collect seismic events, enrich them with location and magnitude context, and provide a clear operational view for analysts.

How It Works

Events are ingested through API, stored in the database, visualized on map/table views, and processed for ShakeMap products.

Why It Helps

A single interface reduces response time, improves consistency, and supports faster operational decisions during seismic activity.

Platform Flow
1) Ingest
Receive earthquake data from SeisComP and internal API sources.
2) Validate
Apply auth, permissions, and input checks before persistence.
3) Process
Run ShakeMap jobs and track status from queue to completion.
4) Deliver
Surface results in event dashboards, maps, and notifications.
